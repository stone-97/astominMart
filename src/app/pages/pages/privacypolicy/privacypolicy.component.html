Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

At Astonic Mart, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with our website, make purchases, or contact our support team.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, delivery address.
  • Payment Details: Transaction information (we DO NOT store card details).
  • Order Information: Items purchased, preferred delivery method.
  • Technical Information: Device type, IP address, browser details.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, interactions on our website, preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Processing and delivering your orders.
  • Providing customer support and communication.
  • Improving our website and shopping experience.
  • Verifying payments and preventing fraudulent transactions.
  • Sending order confirmations, delivery updates, or important notices.

4. Payment Protection & Security

Astonic Mart does not store or have access to your card details. All payments are processed through secure, verified platforms or bank transfers. We only request payment information through our official contact channels.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. However, we may share necessary details with:

  • Trusted logistics partners for delivery purposes.
  • Payment processors for transaction verification.
  • Law enforcement when required by law.

6. Data Storage & Retention

We store your data securely and only for as long as necessary to complete your transactions, comply with legal requirements, or enhance customer support.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies at any time.

8. Customer Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data (where legally possible).
  • Opt-out of non-essential marketing messages.

9. Child Protection

Astonic Mart does not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age. Our services are intended for adults and business users.

10. Data Security Measures

We implement strict security measures including encryption, secure servers, and verification procedures to protect customer data from unauthorized access or misuse.

11. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to external platforms. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We advise customers to review their policies before interacting with them.

12. Updates to This Policy

Astonic Mart may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
